# Canon Changelog

The chronological log of significant canon changes — the canon library's history of record. When a fact changed, what changed it, and why.

Log an entry when: a story reveals something that updates a canon file, an editing pass renames or relocates something, a planning decision is locked or reversed, or a contradiction is found and resolved. Don't log routine additions (a new chapter existing isn't a canon change; what it *reveals* might be).

Newest entries first.
